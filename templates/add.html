Audio Deepfake Detecton

How to Use ?

Audio deepfake detection refers to the process of identifying and mitigating the presence of manipulated or synthetic audio content that is created using advanced machine learning techniques. Deepfake audio technology can generate highly realistic audio clips of people saying things they never actually said, posing a significant challenge to the authenticity and trustworthiness of audio recordings. The term "deepfake" originates from the combination of "deep learning" (a subset of machine learning) and "fake." Deep learning algorithms, particularly variants of generative neural networks like Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) and WaveGANs, are often used to create these manipulated audio clips.
